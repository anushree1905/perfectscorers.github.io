ABOUT THE SAT

Since its debut in 1926, Scholastic Aptitude Test, or SAT has been an integral part of the college admissions process. Especially, since the College Board has suspended SAT Subject Tests, the importance of SAT increases exponentially.

Contrary to what the name suggests, SAT is not a measure of your scholastics or your aptitude. It is a standardized test meant to be tackled with a culmination of smart work and regular practice. What many test takers don't realize is that the College Board follows a standard regimen while making the exam, and therefore, it has to be cracked with a set regimen. And this is exactly where Perfect Scorers comes into play! From providing you with state-of-the-art quality material to conducting regular testing of official SAT papers, we guarantee to guide you to your dream score.
